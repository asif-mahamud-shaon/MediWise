# Images Directory Structure

এই folder-এ নিচের images গুলো save করতে হবে:

## Required Images:

### 1. Hero Section
**File Name:** `hero-doctor.jpg`
**Location:** `/images/hero-doctor.jpg`
**Description:** Professional doctor image (smiling doctor with stethoscope, preferably female doctor)
**Size:** 800x600px বা তার বেশি
**Format:** JPG/PNG

### 2. About Us Section
**File Name:** `about-doctor.jpg`
**Location:** `/images/about-doctor.jpg`
**Description:** Doctor in white lab coat, professional medical setting
**Size:** 600x400px বা তার বেশি
**Format:** JPG/PNG

### 3. Why Choose Us Section
**File Name:** `welcome-doctor.jpg`
**Location:** `/images/welcome-doctor.jpg`
**Description:** Welcoming doctor image (doctor with friendly smile, arms crossed or welcoming gesture)
**Size:** 600x450px বা তার বেশি
**Format:** JPG/PNG

### 4. Doctors Team Section (4 images)
**File Names:**
- `doctors/doctor-1.jpg` - Heart Specialist (Male doctor)
- `doctors/doctor-2.jpg` - Heart Doctor (Male doctor, highlighted)
- `doctors/doctor-3.jpg` - Pediatrician (Female doctor)
- `doctors/doctor-4.jpg` - Neurologist (Male doctor)

**Location:** `/images/doctors/`
**Description:** Professional headshots of doctors (circular format, white background preferred)
**Size:** 300x300px বা তার বেশি (square format)
**Format:** JPG/PNG

### 5. Testimonials Section (3 images)
**File Names:**
- `testimonials/patient-1.jpg` - Christopher Doe (Male patient)
- `testimonials/patient-2.jpg` - Jane Smith (Female patient)
- `testimonials/patient-3.jpg` - Michael Johnson (Male patient)

**Location:** `/images/testimonials/`
**Description:** Patient profile photos (circular format)
**Size:** 200x200px বা তার বেশি (square format)
**Format:** JPG/PNG

### 6. FAQ Section
**File Name:** `faq-team.jpg`
**Location:** `/images/faq-team.jpg`
**Description:** Medical team working together (group of doctors/nurses around a table or computer)
**Size:** 600x400px বা তার বেশি
**Format:** JPG/PNG

### 7. Blog Section (3 images)
**File Names:**
- `blog/blog-1.jpg` - Emergency medicine article
- `blog/blog-2.jpg` - Healthcare technology article
- `blog/blog-3.jpg` - Preventive care article

**Location:** `/images/blog/`
**Description:** Healthcare-related blog post featured images
**Size:** 400x250px বা তার বেশি
**Format:** JPG/PNG

## Image Guidelines:
- সব images professional quality হওয়া উচিত
- High resolution (minimum 800px width for hero images)
- Optimized file size (compress before uploading)
- Consistent style এবং color scheme
- Medical/healthcare theme maintain করুন

## Fallback:
যদি image load না হয়, automatically placeholder SVG show করবে।
