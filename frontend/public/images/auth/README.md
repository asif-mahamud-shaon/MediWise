# Authentication Pages Images

এই folder-এ নিচের images save করতে হবে:

## Required Images:

### 1. Medical Background Image (Main Background)
**File Name:** `medical-background.jpg`
**Location:** `/images/auth/medical-background.jpg`
**Description:** 
- Professional medical/healthcare themed background
- Can be: hospital interior, medical equipment, healthcare professionals working, modern clinic setting
- Should be subtle and not too busy (will have overlay)
- Professional, high-quality photo
- Landscape orientation preferred

**Size:** 1920x1080px বা তার বেশি (full HD)
**Format:** JPG/PNG
**Quality:** High resolution, professional photography
**Style:** Modern, clean, medical/healthcare theme

---

### 2. Pattern Overlay (Optional but Recommended)
**File Name:** `pattern-overlay.png`
**Location:** `/images/auth/pattern-overlay.png`
**Description:**
- Subtle pattern/texture overlay
- Can be: geometric pattern, medical symbols pattern, dots, lines
- Should be transparent PNG with subtle pattern
- Used for adding texture to background

**Size:** 500x500px বা repeatable pattern
**Format:** PNG (with transparency)
**Style:** Subtle, professional pattern

---

### 3. Doctor Image for Login Page
**File Name:** `doctor-login.jpg`
**Location:** `/images/auth/doctor-login.jpg`
**Description:**
- Professional doctor image
- Smiling, friendly expression
- Wearing white lab coat or medical scrubs
- Can be holding stethoscope or medical equipment
- Looking at camera
- Professional, high-quality photo
- Clean/white or blurred background preferred

**Size:** 400x600px বা তার বেশি (portrait orientation)
**Format:** JPG/PNG
**Quality:** High resolution, professional photography

---

### 4. Doctor Image for Register Page
**File Name:** `doctor-register.jpg`
**Location:** `/images/auth/doctor-register.jpg`
**Description:**
- Different professional doctor image (or can be same as login)
- Smiling, friendly expression
- Wearing white lab coat or medical scrubs
- Can be holding stethoscope or medical equipment
- Looking at camera
- Professional, high-quality photo
- Clean/white or blurred background preferred

**Size:** 400x600px বা তার বেশি (portrait orientation)
**Format:** JPG/PNG
**Quality:** High resolution, professional photography

---

## Image Guidelines:

### Background Image:
- Should be professional and medical-themed
- Not too bright or distracting
- Will have dark teal overlay (85% opacity)
- Should work well with white text overlay

### Doctor Images:
- Professional medical setting
- Friendly and approachable expression
- High quality and clear
- Good lighting
- Can be on white/light background or transparent

### Pattern Overlay:
- Very subtle
- Should not interfere with readability
- Professional medical theme (optional)

## Fallback:
যদি images load না হয়, automatically placeholder SVG show করবে।

## Notes:
- সব images high quality হওয়া উচিত
- Professional medical/healthcare theme maintain করতে হবে
- Images optimize করা উচিত (file size manageable)
- Responsive design-এর জন্য images properly sized হওয়া উচিত
